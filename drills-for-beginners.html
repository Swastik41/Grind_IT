Basketball Drills for Beginners – Begin Here!

Simple Easy Training Programs and Tips for Beginners

Warm-Up Drills

  • Jumping Jacks
  • High Knees
  • Arm Circles

30s On / 15s Off

Cycle through jacks, high knees, and butt kicks to elevate heart rate.

Mobility Focus

Hip circles, ankle rolls, and thoracic twists for smooth movement.

Form Check

Stay tall, relax shoulders, breathe through the nose when possible.

Dribbling Drills

  • Stationary Dribbling
  • Walking Dribble
  • Figure Eight Dribble

Low Pound x20

Waist to knee height, quick rhythm, switch hands every 20 reps.

In-&-Out Series

Right in-&-out to cross, left in-&-out to cross. 3 sets each.

Figure-8 Timer

1 minute continuous figure-8, count touches and beat your best.

Passing Drills

  • Chest Pass
  • Bounce Pass
  • Wall Passing

Target Squares

Tape 3 squares on a wall—chest, low, and lead. 20 passes each.

Weak-Hand Focus

10 mins of one-hand push passes (both sides) to build control.

Catch-Ready Hands

Always show a target. Catch with soft hands—absorb, then attack.

Shooting Drills

  • Form Shooting
  • Layup Lines
  • Free Throws

10-Spot Form

Five spots each side of the rim, make 5 at each—focus on arc.

Footwork First

1-2 step into shot; right-left on left side, left-right on right side.

FT Routine

Same breath, same dribble count. 2 shots, then a quick sprint—repeat.

Beginner Basketball Tips

  • Eyes up
  • Stay low
  • Listen to coaches

Bonus: Track makes/misses in a notebook—small wins compound fast.

Fun Drills for Kids

  • Red Light, Green Light
  • Around the World

Easy Basketball Exercises

  • Lunges
  • Jump Rope
  • Push-Ups
