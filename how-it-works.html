The GRIND Basketball Shooting Machine Workings

Simple Setup & Instructions

  1. Easy Setup – Unfold the rebounder, attach the net, and position it behind the hoop.
  2. Position for Training – Adjust the net angle and shooting spots.
  3. Practice Alone or with Team – Automatic rebounding keeps drills going.
  4. Track Progress & Improve – Focus on form, speed, and accuracy.
  5. Easy Breakdown and Storage – Fold and store in minutes.

Tips for Best Results

  • Mix shot types: catch-and-shoot, off-dribble, free throws.
  • Track your makes and misses.
  • Adjust angles to target weaknesses.
